About me

I am a passionate software development disguised as a game developer with a deep interest in systems programming,
game development, and low-level technologies. Over the years, I've honed my skills through hands-on experience,
working on various projects, such as game engines, emulators, and compilers/interpreter.

My approach to development focuses on understanding how things work at their core; Whether it's recreating classic hardware behaviour or optimising performance in modern software.

I'm constantly learning and exploring new technologies. When I'm not coding, you'll likely find me researching new trends in system designs or playing retro games for inspiration.
